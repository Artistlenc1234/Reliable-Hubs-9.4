Floor Standing Server/Networking Racks

Floor Standing Server/Networking Racks are an economical and versatile solution for contractors, small computer rooms, schools, and enterprise networks that need dependable housing for servers and communication equipment.

Mounting Standard
19" DIN mounting angles with square slots for M6 cage nuts
Ranges
17U to 48U with 650, 800, 1000, 1200 and 1300 mm depths
Load Capacity
Rack frame supports up to 1200 kg with 3 pairs of support channels

Features

  • Perforated, glass, and mesh door options with reversible hinges

  • Heavy-duty castor set with 2 braking and 2 non-braking wheels
  • Base plinth with grouting facility for stable floor anchoring
  • Heavy-duty M12 leveling legs with check nuts
  • Optional accessories: fixed shelf, sliding shelf, keyboard drawer, support angles
  • Powder-coated texture finish for durability and aesthetic consistency
  • Cost-effective and service-friendly rack solution for servers and networking hardware
