Mezzanine Racking

Mezzanine racking is a storage solution that incorporates a mezzanine floor structure, effectively doubling or tripling the usable space within a warehouse by creating additional levels above the ground floor. This system is ideal for optimizing vertical space and is suitable for various types of storage, including palletized goods, cartons, and bulk items. Mezzanine racking is versatile and can be customized to fit different warehouse layouts and storage requirements.

Storage Type
Multi-level storage for pallets, cartons, and bulk goods
Access
Multiple levels accessible by staircases, lifts, or conveyors
Flexibility
Customizable floor heights and layouts for varied needs

Features

  • High-Density Storage: Maximizes vertical space, significantly increasing capacity without expanding the warehouse footprint.
  • Multi-Level Access: Provides several tiers of storage accessible via integrated staircases, goods lifts, or conveyor systems.
  • Versatility: Suitable for a wide range of products, from small parts and documents to larger, bulky items.
  • Customizable: Can be tailored to meet specific requirements, including varying shelf heights and structural configurations.
  • Improved Organization: Facilitates a systematic layout, enhancing inventory management and item retrieval speed.
  • Cost-Effective: Increases storage capacity without the need for significant structural changes or warehouse expansion.
  • Flexibility: Easily adaptable to changing business needs; can be reconfigured or expanded as required.
  • Safety Features: Equipped with handrails, guardrails, and anti-slip flooring to ensure a secure working environment.
