Longspan Shelving

Longspan shelving, also known as wide span shelving or industrial shelving, is a versatile and robust storage solution designed for medium to heavy-duty applications. It provides efficient storage for bulky and large items that may not fit on traditional shelving units, offering a balance between the strength of pallet racking and the accessibility of conventional shelving.
