Carton Live Shelving

Carton live racking, also known as gravity flow racking, is a dynamic storage system designed for high-density storage and efficient order picking of cartons, bins, and other non-palletized goods. It uses inclined roller or wheel tracks to move goods by gravity from the loading end to the picking end, supporting high-throughput operations and FIFO inventory flow.

Carton live shelving built for FIFO flow and high-throughput picking

Carton live shelving improves order fulfilment by using gravity-driven flow lanes that move products from replenishment to picking points efficiently. This reduces manual handling, supports high-density storage, and keeps inventory clearly organized for faster access.

Designed for FIFO inventory management and high-throughput environments, it is widely used in distribution centres, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, retail warehousing, and food and beverage operations where speed, control, and space optimization are critical.
