Mobile Motorized Racking

Mobile motorized racking, also known as mobile shelving or mobile racking, is an advanced storage solution designed to maximize storage density and optimize space utilization. Racking units are mounted on motorized bases that move along tracks to open aisles on demand, significantly increasing capacity by removing multiple fixed aisles.

Mobile motorized racking built for maximum density and on-demand access

Mobile motorized racking uses track-mounted motorized bases to move storage rows and open aisles only where access is needed. This approach can deliver up to 80% more usable storage compared with conventional fixed-aisle racking, making it highly effective for space-constrained facilities.

By combining high storage density, flexible layout options, and WMS-ready automation, the system improves organization, reduces handling effort, and lowers long-term operating costs across warehouses, archives, manufacturing, retail, and temperature-controlled environments.
