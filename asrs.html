Automated Storage & Retrieval System (ASRS)

An Automated Storage and Retrieval System (AS/RS) is a sophisticated warehouse storage solution that automates storing and retrieving goods through robotics, computer-controlled systems, and software. It is built for high-density storage and fast throughput across product types ranging from small parts to full pallets.
