Drive-in Racking

Drive-in racking is a high-density storage system designed to maximize warehouse space by minimizing aisle requirements, allowing forklifts to drive directly into the racking structure to place and retrieve pallets.

Storage Type
High-density pallet storage for bulk applications
Depth

  • Improves space efficiency in facilities with limited floor area
  • Cost-effective for bulk storage and lower overhead requirements

Product Walkthrough

Drive-in racking built for bulk storage and higher warehouse density

Drive-in racking is designed to maximize warehouse capacity by reducing aisle space and allowing forklifts to enter the rack structure directly. This makes it a strong solution for facilities that need to store large volumes of homogeneous products with low SKU variation.

Its deep-lane design, durable construction, and suitability for LIFO storage make it especially effective in cold storage, food and beverage, manufacturing, and bulk inventory environments where high-density storage is a priority.
