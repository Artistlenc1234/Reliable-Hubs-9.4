Multi-Tier Racking

Multi-tier racking, also known as multi-level racking, is a storage solution designed to maximize vertical space by creating multiple levels of racking for lightweight to medium-weight manually handled goods.

Storage Type
Multi-level storage for cartons, boxes, and medium-weight goods
Access
Multiple levels accessible by staircases, lifts, or conveyors
Flexibility
Customizable shelf heights and configurations for varied storage needs

Features

  • High-density storage maximizes vertical space and increases capacity
  • Multi-level access supports organized retrieval across several tiers
  • Versatile enough for small parts, cartons, boxes, and larger manually handled items
  • Customizable layouts adapt to changing storage requirements
  • Improved organization enhances inventory management and access speed
  • Cost-effective way to expand storage without major structural changes
  • Flexible design can be reconfigured or expanded as needed
  • Safety features such as handrails, guardrails, and anti-slip flooring support safer operations
Ideal for warehouses, distribution centres, manufacturing, and retail stockrooms.

Multi-tier racking built for vertical space optimization and organized access

Multi-tier racking makes the most of available vertical space by creating several levels of storage within the same footprint. It is especially useful for facilities that store cartons, boxes, overstock items, documents, and other manually handled goods that benefit from organized multi-level access.

With adaptable layouts, strong safety features, and the ability to improve organization and retrieval efficiency, multi-tier racking provides a practical and cost-effective way to increase storage capacity across warehouses, distribution centres, retail backrooms, manufacturing sites, and archive facilities.
